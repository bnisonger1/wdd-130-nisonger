Solace Counseling is dedicated to bringing therapeutic healing to couples, individuals, and families in our community through evidence-based psychotherapy. We are currently accepting new clients. Please visit our patient portal to schedule an appointment here.

Welcome! I became a counselor because I am passionate about the idea that strong relationships build strong communities. It is my honor to work with clients to achieve their goals through person-centered, customized, and evidence-based therapeutic approaches. I grew up in a military family and love putting down roots in beautiful Alaska with my husband and three children.

Education, Training, and Experience: I received my Master’s Degree of Science (MS) in Marriage and Family Therapy from Capella University in 2020. I received board certification as a Nationally Certified Counselor (NCC) in 2021 and as a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist (LMFT) in 2022. I have received post-graduate training in the Gottman Method for counseling couples, EMDR for processing trauma, multicultural counseling, grief, and telehealth. I have worked in the mental health field since 2017.

Areas of Specialization and Services Available: Solace Counseling welcomes all client populations ages 13 and up. Couples, individual, and family counseling are available. For questions about treatment options available for clients under the age of 13, please contact me at e.nisonger@solacecounselingak.com. Treatment approaches are customized to meet the needs of the client. Solution Focused Therapy , Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Person-Centered Therapy, The Gottman Method, and Existential Therapy are some treatments utilized. I have experience treating couples’ issues, anxiety, depression, attachment issues, trauma, PTSD, complex PTSD, change of life issues, psychosis, LGBTQ+ issues, OCD, parent child conflict, adolescents, and grief. Christian counseling is available upon request.
